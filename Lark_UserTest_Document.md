### What is Lark?

Lark is a personal bucket list app for places and experiences. It helps you save things you want to do — and then recommends what to actually go and do based on the weather, your mood, how you're travelling, and how much time you have.

---

### Before you start

- Open the app at: **wandermind-wine.vercel.app**
- Use a real place or two that you know — not made-up ones
- Try to work through the tasks in order
- If something confuses you, note it down — that's exactly what we want to know
- There are no wrong answers

---

### Your Tasks

**Task 1 — Create an account and set up your profile**
Sign up with your email and password. After signing up, **check your inbox for a confirmation email from Lark and click the link inside** — this is required before you can log in. Once confirmed, return to the app at **wandermind-wine.vercel.app** and log in with your email and password. If you see an error message on the page after clicking the link, don't worry — just go back to the app and log in manually. Once logged in, you'll be asked for your name and your home location (used to calculate travel times). Complete the setup until you reach the main screen.

**Task 2 — Add places you'd like to visit**
Add at least 3 places to your bucket list — these can be anywhere: a restaurant, a hiking trail, a museum, a city, anything. Search for them by name and add them.

**Task 3 — Add a place you've already been**
Think of somewhere you've visited before that you'd consider a highlight. Add it to your list, then find it and mark it as done. Once marked, open the review and leave a short note about the experience.

**Task 4 — Get a recommendation**
From the main screen, tap the recommendation button. Work through the steps (date, time available, who you're with, mood, how you're getting there, etc.) and see what Lark suggests for you.

**Task 5 — Explore a recommended place**
Open one of the results from Task 4 and read through its detail page.
