### What is Lark?

Lark is a personal bucket list app for places and experiences. It helps you save things you want to do, and then recommends what to actually go and do based on the weather, your mood, how you're travelling, and how much time you have.

---

### Before you start

Open the app at: **wandermind-wine.vercel.app**

We recommend installing Lark on your phone first (it only takes a few seconds), but it also works fine in your normal browser. Use a real place or two that you know, not made-up ones. Try to work through the tasks in order. If something confuses you, note it down. That is exactly what we want to know. There are no wrong answers.

---

### Returning testers (round 1 participants)

Welcome back, and thank you. Please **skip Task 1 (sign-up and onboarding)** and just log in with your existing account. On the feedback form, skip **questions 1, 2 and 6** (the sign-up questions in Section 1). Everything else has had improvements since round 1, including search, photos, the recommend flow and the dashboard, so please do still install the app and work through the rest of the tasks and questions as if it were your first time.

### Optional: Install Lark on your phone

Lark is a Progressive Web App, which means you can add it to your home screen and use it like a normal app, with its own icon and no browser bar. It is the best way to experience it, but skipping this step is fine if you prefer.

**On iPhone (Safari):**

1. Open **wandermind-wine.vercel.app** in **Safari** (not Chrome, this only works in Safari on iOS).
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of the screen).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top right. Lark will appear on your home screen with its own icon.

**On Android (Chrome):**

1. Open **wandermind-wine.vercel.app** in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots** menu in the top right corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**, depending on your device).
4. Tap **Add** or **Install** to confirm. Lark will appear on your home screen with its own icon.

If a prompt to install pops up automatically, you can just tap that instead.

### Your Tasks

**Task 1: Create an account and set up your profile**
Sign up with your email and password. After signing up, check your inbox for a confirmation email from Lark and click the link inside. This is required before you can log in. Once confirmed, return to the app at **wandermind-wine.vercel.app** and log in with your email and password. If you see an error message on the page after clicking the link, do not worry, just go back to the app and log in manually. Once logged in, you will be asked for your name and your home location (used to calculate travel times). Complete the setup until you reach the main screen.

**Task 2: Add places you would like to visit**
Add at least 3 places to your bucket list. These can be anywhere: a restaurant, a hiking trail, a museum, a city, anything. Search for them by name and add them.

**Task 3: Add a place by pasting a Google Maps link**
On the Add a place screen, look for the option to paste a Google Maps link. Open Google Maps (in a browser or in the mobile app), find a place, copy its share link, and paste it into Lark. Both full desktop links and the shortened mobile share links (maps.app.goo.gl/...) should work. Check that the right place comes through.

**Task 4: Add a place you have already been**
Think of somewhere you have visited before that you would consider a highlight. Add it to your list, then find it and mark it as done. Once marked, open the review and leave a short note about the experience.

**Task 5: Get a recommendation**
From the main screen, tap the recommendation button. Work through the steps (date, time available, who you are with, mood, how you are getting there, etc.) and see what Lark suggests for you.

**Task 6: Explore the Dashboard rails**
Back on the main screen, scroll through the horizontal rows ("rails") underneath the main buttons. You should see things like "Perfect for today", "Quick wins", "Top of your list", and rails for your biggest categories. Tap a "See all" link on one of them and see where it takes you.

**Task 7: Try the Discover feed**
Find the "Discover nearby" rail on the main screen and tap "See all" to open the full Discover screen. Browse the places suggested there (these come from notable places nearby plus places other Lark testers have saved). Tap one that interests you and add it to your list.

**Task 8: Check the privacy toggle in Settings**
Go to Settings and find the "Share my saves anonymously" toggle. Read what it says and decide whether you want it on or off. There is no right answer, we just want to know how the wording lands.

**Task 9: Explore a recommended place**
Open one of the results from Task 5 (or any place on your list) and read through its detail page. Look at the photos, opening hours, and description.
